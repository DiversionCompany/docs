<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web UI">
    Inviting someone to collaborate on a repo is simple:

    <Steps>
        <Step title="Navigate to the repos page">
            Click the sloth icon at the top left corner, or [click here](https://app.diversion.dev)
        </Step>
        <Step title="Open the repo settings">
            Click the gear icon next to the repo name
            <img src="/images/repo-settings.jpg" alt="Repo Settings" style={{width: '90%', borderRadius: '1.5rem', border: '.3rem solid #555', boxShadow: '0 0 1rem #888' }} />
        </Step>
        <Step title="Invite via email">
            Enter your collaborator's email at the bottom of the modal, select the access level to grant, and click Send invite
        </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="CLI">
    Inviting someone to collaborate on a repo is simple even from the CLI:
    ```bash
    dv invite user@example.org
    ```
    which will grant write access to the repo to the invitee. To share with different permissions run:
    ```bash
    dv invite -access [READ|WRITE|ADMIN|OWNER] user@example.org
    ```
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
